Frontliners

It Doesn't Take a Cape or Superpowers to Be a Hero

I would like to express my deepest gratitude to all the frontliners who faced life and death situations every now and then. I thank you for your hard-work and perseverance to continue fighting against the spreading virus. Your selfless service to the world is helping us all get through this crisis. Words are not enough to thank you for your strength, courage, and dedication to give service to those in need. I know that being on the frontline isn't an easy job, but all of us appreciate it and all of you deserve our applause, our thanks, and our respect. All of us are deeply grateful for the sacrifices that you have been making throughout this pandemic.

Thank You So Much!
